This Roque qui Chante is a great stone, standing in a field near Mons. Lemézurier de Fry's. It is a stone of a highly suspicious character. No one knows what deeds are done around it. At times you may hear there a cock crowing, when no cock is near--an extremely disagreeable circumstance. Then it is commonly asserted that this stone was originally placed in the field by the elfin people known as _Sarregousets_, who are the same as the _Sins_.
