When the boat was raised, and slung again to the davits, the _Tamaulipas_ was in motion once more. The land-breeze was fresh, and she spread all her sails. Clubin's glass continued fixed upon her outline growing more and more indistinct; until half an hour later, when the _Tamaulipas_ had become only a dark shape upon the horizon, growing smaller and smaller against the pale twilight in the sky.
