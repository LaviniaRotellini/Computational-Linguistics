They were unanimous in their admiration of Clubin; unanimous also in believing him to be saved after all. The _Shealtiel_ cutter had arrived some hours after the _Cashmere_. It was this vessel which had brought the last items of intelligence. She had passed four-and-twenty hours in the same waters as the Durande. She had lain-to in the fog, and tacked about during the squall. The captain of the _Shealtiel_ was present among the company.
