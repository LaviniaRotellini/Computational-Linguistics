She, no less pale, no less like a statue, had hardly noticed that they had placed in her hand a heavy, lighted candle of yellow wax; she had not heard the yelping voice of the clerk reading the fatal contents of the apology; when they told her to respond with _Amen_, she responded _Amen_. She only recovered life and force when she beheld the priest make a sign to her guards to withdraw, and himself advance alone towards her.
