They declared themselves the Friends of the A B C,—the _Abaissé_,—the debased,—that is to say, the people. They wished to elevate the people. It was a pun which we should do wrong to smile at. Puns are sometimes serious factors in politics; witness the _Castratus ad castra_, which made a general of the army of Narses; witness: _Barbari et Barberini_; witness: _Tu es Petrus et super hanc petram_, etc., etc.
